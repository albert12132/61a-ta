Q2

NOTE: we are using scheme_eval here, which behaves different than calc_eval from Discussion 10. Make sure you know the difference, and how that affects the number of times eval and apply are called!

    scm> 4
    eval: 1
    apply: 0
    scm> (+ 3 (+ 5 2))
    eval: 7
    apply: 2
    scm> (double 4)
    eval: 7
    apply: 2
    scm> (double (double 4))
    eval: 13
    apply: 4
    scm> (and 2 #f 4)
    eval: 4
    apply: 0
